How to Play

No sign-up, no rules to memorize — settle the debate in a few taps.

1

Pick a tournament

Choose any bracket above — your favorite band's 64 best songs, ready to battle.

2

Tap to advance

In each matchup, tap the song you'd keep. Work through every round until all four regions have a winner.

3

Crown a champion

Set your Final Four, pick the one that takes the crown, then share it or see how your picks rank on the leaderboard.

In a hurry? Start with an Auto-Fill mode, then tweak
🎲 Random — fills the whole bracket instantly.
📊 Most Streamed — seeds by Spotify play counts.
🎤 Most Played Live — seeds by real concert frequency.
🌟 Highest Seed — our fan-weighted seeding takes over.
🏆 Crowd's Picks — every matchup decided by what fans actually vote.
❤️ Your Favorites — lock in your top songs as #1 seeds.
🔥 Controversy — underdogs run wild in round one.
🎵 My Concert — build it from the real setlist of a show you attended.
