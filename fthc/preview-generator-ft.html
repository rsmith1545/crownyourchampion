🎵 Preview Link Generator — Frank Turner

Run this once to bake the song previews into your Frank Turner site so every device (including iPhones) can play them.

  1. Click Generate Links below and let it run (about 2–3 minutes — it paces itself so Apple doesn’t block it).
  2. When it finishes, click Download preview-urls.json.
  3. Upload preview-urls.json to your Frank Turner site, in the same folder as its index.html.
  4. Reload the site. Done — previews now play everywhere with no live lookups.

Tip: if it finds only a few, your network may be temporarily rate-limited — wait ~15 minutes (or try different WiFi) and run it again. A handful of brand-new 2026 songs may not have iTunes previews yet; that’s normal.
