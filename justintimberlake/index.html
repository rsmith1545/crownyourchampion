How to Play

1. Pick your way to a champion. Tap a song in each matchup to advance it. The part most people miss: the center Championship box stays locked until you work through the four regions — fill those out and the Final Four (then the final) unlock in the middle. Crown your winner there.

2. Forget or don't know a song? Hit ▶ on any song for a quick preview — or on a phone, press & hold any song (works in every round). Great for jogging your memory or comparing two you don't know.

3. Want a head start? Tap an Auto-Fill button up top to fill the whole bracket instantly, then change any pick you disagree with.

Auto-Fill modes
📊 Most Streamed — the most-streamed songs on Spotify advance.
🎤 Most Played Live — the songs played most in concert advance.

🏆 Crowd's Picks — every matchup decided by what fans actually vote.
