Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Crown Your Champion is an independent fan project. Here's what the site collects and how it's used — plain and short.

What we collect

  • Traffic analytics (Google Analytics 4). Like most websites, we use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit, which brackets are popular, and where visitors come from. GA4 sets cookies and gathers anonymized usage data — pages viewed, approximate location (country/region), and device type. We use this only to improve the site and decide which tournaments to build next. We don't use it to identify you personally.
  • Email signups (optional). If you enter your email to be notified about new brackets, we store it (using Google Firebase) along with the date and, if you provided one, the band you requested. We use it only to email you about new tournaments. We never sell or share it, and you can unsubscribe anytime.
  • Local browser storage. The site saves your bracket progress and a few preferences in your own browser so your picks aren't lost. This stays on your device.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • We don't show ads or use advertising trackers.
  • We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies for traffic measurement. You can block or delete cookies anytime in your browser settings — the site still works without them. You'll see a one-time notice about this when you first visit.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe or delete your email: email us (below) and we'll remove you promptly.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics: install Google's browser opt-out add-on, or block analytics cookies in your browser.

Contact

Questions, or want your data removed? Email crownyourchampion@gmail.com.

Crown Your Champion is an independent fan project, not affiliated with or endorsed by any artist. This policy is provided in good faith and in plain language; it is not legal advice.
