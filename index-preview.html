How to Play

No sign-up, no rules to memorize — settle the debate in a few taps.

1

Pick a tournament

Choose any bracket above — your favorite band's 64 best songs, ready to battle.

2

Tap to advance

In each matchup, tap the song you'd keep. Work through every round until all four regions have a winner.

3

Crown a champion

Set your Final Four, pick the one that takes the crown, then share it or see how your picks rank on the leaderboard.
